Thesis (Mechanical Lifter Design)

My group calculated, designed, and simulated a mechanical lifter that's purpose was to improve the ergonomics of the lifting process in a manufacturing plant.

Thesis (Ergonomics)

I handled majority of the ergonomics aspect of our thesis such as the determination of optimal lifting height, as well as the initial and final ergonomic calculations and comparison. The calculations mainly used the National Institute for Occupational Safety & Health (NIOSH) Lifting Equation.

Simulations (Stress and Flow Simulations)

Conducted simulations in Solidworks (Fluid Flow Simulations) to determine the best ventilation within a CPU and ANSYS (Stress Simulations) for my thesis.

SolidWorks (3D Design)

Had multiple 3D Designs using SolidWORKS, one example is designing a rocket ship with multiple fins. The rockets were then simulated in a wind tunnel to determine which number of fins was more aerodynamically efficient.

Autocad (2D Design)

Had multiple projects done in Autocad, whether it be frameworking, dimensioning, layouting, or drafting.

Bibliographic Paper (Research Assistant)

Created a literature review paper regarding Hybrid Renewable Energy Systems (HRES). The paper contained 70+ scientific research papers and compared them in terms of RE systems, optimization technique, geographic location, components, cost, etc.
